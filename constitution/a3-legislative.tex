\article{USG Parliament}
\label{USGParliamentDef}
The USG Parliament shall have the role to coordinate the USG, to explicitly define its tasks and to distribute workload. The \hyperref[USGexecutiveUnitDef]{USG Executive Unit} shall \change[S]{fulfill the proposals of}{take up tasks delegated to them by} the USG Parliament, upon common agreement between the two bodies.
